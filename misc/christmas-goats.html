christmas goats

home

2023

the original plan was to crochet a goat using a pattern shared on the goat's twitter account , but a test run on different yarn showed it was going to be too big. the next plan was to just play it by ear on the dimensions, but unfortunately we had a burst pipe and had to move things around at home and our cotton twine got lost just in time for things to shut down for the christmas break.

however, inspired by a trip to wētā workshop in new zealand, i decided to make a skeleton out of aluminium foil and fill it with some kind of paper—especially fitting as the real-life goat, being consumed by jackdaws, had by this point been picked apart enough to have a visible skeleton herself.
