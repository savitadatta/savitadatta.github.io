whenever I mention learning to spin wool to anyone, the question is the same: what will you do with the wool? taking into account the history of the wool itself, it's a no-brainer: I'm going to make some gloves for my dad.

the yarn

I've made a 3ply yarn for these; no idea what length, as I'm spinning and crocheting in parallel for that sweet sweet ✨variety✨.
