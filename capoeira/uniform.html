june 2023

so I've been going to capoeira on and off (mostly off) for a couple years now, but I haven't really followed up on getting a uniform for it (due to being painfully awkward)—I'll spare you the details but finally got around to asking about it!

the thing is, for adjustability the pants come super long, so I needed to shorten them so they wouldn't be a tripping hazard.

alterations

the needle!

as mentioned in previous posts, I needed to get some needles for working with stretch fabric. Spotlight conveniently had a sale on sewing machine needles, so I picked some up the other day!

getting to it

honestly I have no idea of the ideal length so I just made sure the fold was off the ground, measured an approximate length to cut off (about the length of my index finger, or roughly 7cm), and sewed a double hem—only made one significant mistake in folding the material to the wrong side but luckily it was an easy fix!

how's the needle?

well, it didn't seem to have any major difficulties! so, about the best you can expect, really.
