the ice cream

ingredients

  • 400ml coconut milk
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • about ¾ of a 270ml can of coconut cream (i didn't measure this at all)
  • 6? kaffir lime leaves (2 whole, the rest chopped)
  • about a thumb-sized (my thumb) amount of ginger (frozen and thawed because that's what we had)
  • a loose handful of coriander
  • the zest of about a quarter of a kaffir lime (more if you have more patience than i do)
  • 1.5ish tbsp green curry paste
  • 1.5ish tsp lemongrass paste

method

  1. combine the coconut cream and sugar together until the sugar has dissolved
  2. put in the fridge cos the rest is going to take a while
  3. combine the rest (sans lemongrass paste because i forgot it was there) in a saucepan
  4. heat up, let simmer for a little bit, then let it sit all together in the fridge for a bit
  5. strain the stuff out of the coconut milk; combine with the cream
  6. (optional: keep the pulp to marinate other stuff with later)
  7. freeze, whisking occasionally (or use an ice cream maker)

the pawpaw

ingredients

i probably could have just included it raw but the futzing goblin on my shoulder was like 'we should do something to this'

  • ¼ of a red pawpaw, blended (∼400ml)
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • the tiniest smidge of kaffir lime juice (i used a quarter of one and had to add the rest of the puree in to cancel it out)

method

  1. combine in a pan
  2. heat up
  3. cool in the fridge
  4. fold in to the rest once they're both ready

results

this tastes bomb as hell and you should absolutely make some
