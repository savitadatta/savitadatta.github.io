sunflower

home

probably april 2024

i was growing sunflowers out the front of the house and harvested a few of the heads way too early, but as i was taking the little flowers off to check the seeds they smelled really lovely, so i made a syrup and asked for suggestions on my instagram story, and two people suggested i make ice cream! so here we are

the syrup

  • the little flowers on the face of the sunflower heads
  • the green pointy leaves behind the head (couldn't be bothered to take them out)
  • water, until the flower bits were covered (1.5 cups)
  • sugar to match the water (1.5 cups)

heated it up, let it sit, refrigerated it, took the solids out the next day

the ice cream

this will once again end up more of a semifreddo because i don't have an ice cream churner and don't want to have to keep taking it out and whisking it as it freezes

ingredients (approximate because i forgot to write it down)

  • ⅓ cup sunflower syrup
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • vanilla to taste
  • extra sunflower syrup to taste
all ice cream
