peach and strawberry

home

6 january 2025; recipe from 31 december 2024

ingredients

  • 3 white flesh peaches
  • about a third of a punnet of strawberries
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1½ cup heavy cream
  • a pinch of cream of tartar
  • vanilla to taste

method

  1. blend the fruit together
  2. double boil the yolks and brown sugar, whisking until warm and combined
  3. make the meringue
  4. make the whipped cream
  5. realise that the yolks have been sitting on the steam the whole time
  6. bemoan the fact that there's no dog in the house any more to enjoy the extra egg
  7. think about how many more ways than expected a dog's presence is felt in a house
  8. think about other ways dogs are present in the running of a household
  9. think about how quiet it's been
  10. think about how long it's been since there were no dogs in the house
  11. think about how much future there is left without her around
  12. get more eggs
  13. do the yolk bit again with new eggs
  14. combine a normal amount of each component
  15. freeze
  16. enjoy
