in december we had the oddities and curiosities expo in town, and with them were some people from sleeping sirens leading a taxidermy workshop—two, actually—one on a rabbit, and one on a rat, but i only went to the rabbit one. some of the steps are detailed below.

general process

  1. remove & tan the hide (already done)
  2. create shape for head out of florists' foam
  3. make eye socket dents, fill with clay & place eyes
  4. shape body; stick wires through where the legs come out (loop other ends around so it catches in the foam)
  5. wrap polyfill around legs; wrap twine around polyfill to keep it in place
  6. use a stick to attach head to body
  7. take out some foam at the mouth; fill with clay
  8. ensure hide is dry and brushed on outside (but don't hairdry the face yet as it'll shrink)
  9. roll hide over foam creature
  10. fill legs
  11. sew hide together at spine
  12. position on mount
  13. card ears; fix face details; pin
  14. wait a fortnight
all (laboratory)
